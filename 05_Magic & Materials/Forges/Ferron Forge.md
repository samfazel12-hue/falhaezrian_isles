**Region:** [[Béornic]] Heartland
**Type:** forge settlement
**Status:** active

## Overview

Ferron Forge produces magically infused steel in multiple colors. The colors mark bound spellwork, not decoration.

## Geography and Access

In the [[Béornic]] heartland near the eastern craft and military sphere.

## Economy and Function

Enchanted blades, colored steel, military-grade metalwork.
