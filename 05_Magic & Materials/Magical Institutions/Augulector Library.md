**Region:** [[Meritia]]
**Type:** library / learned institution
**Status:** active

## Overview

The Augulector Library is one of the greatest learned institutions in the Isles. It stands on [[Meritia]] and is now defended by [[House Godbrandt]].

## Geography and Access

On [[Meritia]] in the [[Great Pearl]].

## Economy and Function

Scholarship, records, copying, learned authority.

## Politics and Power

Control of the library gives [[House Godbrandt]] legitimacy despite Meritian displacement.
