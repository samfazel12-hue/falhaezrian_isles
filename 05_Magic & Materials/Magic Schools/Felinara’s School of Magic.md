# Felinara’s School

**Region:** [[Kazaros]]
**Type:** school of magic
**Status:** active

## Overview

Felinara’s School teaches gem, crystal, and mineral-based magic. Its discipline is material, precise, and mountain-derived rather than nature-bound.

## Geography and Access

Draws on [[Kazaros]] mineral matter and mountain access.

## Economy and Function

Magical education, gemcraft, mineral spellwork.
