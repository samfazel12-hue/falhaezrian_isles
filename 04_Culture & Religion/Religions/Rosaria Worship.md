**Pantheon:** [[Rosaria]] / older Sentran land-sacred tradition
**Worshipped by:** Sentran communities, old nature-bound traditions
**Sacred sites:** rivers, lakes, hills, mountains, tide, rain, sacred landscapes

## Overview

[[Rosaria]] worship concerns the living natural order of [[Sentra]]. It predates or underlies much of the later [[Béornic]] political myth and makes the land itself sacred.

## Core Beliefs and Practices

Rivers, lakes, hills, mountains, tide, rain, and life of [[Sentra]] are not symbols only; they are expressions of [[Rosaria]]’s presence.

## Relation to Political Power

The House uses [[Rosaria]] through the Bloodroyal myth, but this creates tension because [[Béorn]]’s taking of her power is morally and theologically charged.

## History

[[Rosaria]] became partner/captive of [[Béorn]] and mother of the [[Bloodroyals]]. Her inheritance shaped founder houses and magical lines.
