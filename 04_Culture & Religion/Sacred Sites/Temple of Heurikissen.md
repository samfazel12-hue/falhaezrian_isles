**Region:** [[Meritia]]
**Type:** religious institution
**Status:** active

## Overview

The Temple of Heurikissen is a major sacred foundation on [[Meritia]], founded through Augus’s learned and holy legacy.

## Geography and Access

On [[Meritia]], near or within the island’s sacred scholarly complex.

## Economy and Function

Pilgrimage, teaching, ritual, temple lands.

## Politics and Power

Godbrandt control over the temple gives them religious authority but also exposes them to Meritian resentment.
