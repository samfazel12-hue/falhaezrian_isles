**Date:** c. 2140–2100 BHK
**Location:** [[Sentra]]
**Era:** Late Pre-Unification Age

## Summary

[[Béorn]] unified [[Sentra]] through alliance, war, incorporation, and sacred kingship. This ended the age of tribal fragmentation and created the civilizational center of the later Isles.

## Causes

[[Sentra]] was divided among tribes, clans, petty kings, and warlords. [[Béorn]]’s growing power and alliances allowed him to force or negotiate a new order.

## Course of Events

[[Béorn]] allied with the Men of Mountain and Lake, brought in the Clans of Hills and Rivers and the Men of Bay and Vulcan, and defeated Krova at Twixtriver.

## Outcome

[[Béorn]] became King of [[Sentra]] in 2099 BHK.

## Consequences

Founder houses, [[House of Béorn]], sacred kingship, and later council politics all rest on this unification.

## Key Figures

- [[Béorn]]
- [[Krova “Kingkiller” Yarrowgold]]
- [[Rosaria]]

## Contradictions / Open Issues

Exact sequence of tribal incorporation is still loose.

## Who Benefited

[[Béorn]], the later House order, incorporated allies.

## Who Lost

Krova, independent petty rulers, rival warlords.

## Disputed Interpretations

[[Béornic]] tradition frames it as civilization; conquered peoples may remember it as domination.
