**Date:** 10,099 AHK onward
**Location:** [[Falhaezrian Isles]]
**Era:** [[Continental Invasions]]

## Summary

The First Continental Invasion overran much of the archipelago beyond the eastern core. It forced broader unity under the [[High King]] in exchange for liberation and defense.

## Causes

Continental imperial expansion and the Isles’ vulnerability outside the eastern core.

## Course of Events

The Empire gained ground across the archipelago. The eastern core resisted, then the islanders drove the invaders back through magic, arms, command, and maritime coordination.

## Outcome

The Isles survived and counter-invaded the mainland, but could not sustain deep inland war.

## Consequences

Tribute, allegiance, military duty, port oversight, and crown authority deepened across the Isles.
