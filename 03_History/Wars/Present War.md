**Date:** 12,064–12,076 AHK
**Location:** Continent and [[Falhaezrian Isles]]
**Era:** Present Age

## Summary

The present war is the current overseas conflict against the [[Continental Empire]], intended to break it as a permanent threat. Early gains have slowed into a brutal mainland war under a new, more severe [[High King]].

## Causes

Long memory of Continental invasions, pirate pressure, maritime insecurity, and the House’s strategic decision to destroy the Empire rather than merely defend against it.

## Course of Events

The Isles counterstruck after the [[Third Continental Invasion]]. The old [[High King]] died during the war; his successor accelerated the campaign. Ports and movement across the Isles came under emergency control.

## Outcome

War continues in the present year, 12,076 AHK.

## Consequences

Centralization, restricted travel, port supervision, [[Kallindra]] overload, tax pressure, and regional resentment.
