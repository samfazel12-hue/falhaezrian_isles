**Date:** c. 11,130 AHK
**Location:** [[Falhaezrian Isles]]
**Era:** [[Continental Invasions]]

## Summary

The Second Continental Invasion struck a more unified and prepared Isles. It failed quickly compared with the first, but still left bitter wounds.

## Causes

Renewed Continental attempt to break the Isles.

## Course of Events

The invaders attacked but were repelled within months.

## Outcome

The Empire failed to secure a lasting foothold.

## Consequences

[[Lonely Rock]] was sacked. The Isles counterattacked but were too spent to continue. The aftermath helped fracture the realm.
