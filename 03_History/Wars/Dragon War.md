**Date:** c. 3600–3900 AHK
**Location:** [[Falhaezrian Isles]]; [[Mount Vulka]]; [[Broken Forest]]
**Era:** Dragon War

## Summary

Dragons emerged through volcanic routes and tore through the political and economic order of the Isles. The war left dragonfire glass plains, serpentglass, and enduring hatred of dragonborn in some regions.

## Causes

The true cause lies in blood magic, not natural emergence. Dragons entered through Vulka and the Underdark volcanic connection.

## Course of Events

Dragons destroyed banks, kings, houses, wealth centers, settlements, holds, and forests. One dragon devastated the [[Broken Forest]] and was finally resisted at [[Last Stand]].

## Outcome

Many dragons were slain, but some were never accounted for.

## Consequences

The [[Broken Forest]] remains ecologically compromised. [[Culverno]]’s serpentglass economy exists because of dragonfire-vitrified soil.
