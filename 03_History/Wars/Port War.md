**Date:** before and during [[Present War]]
**Location:** Falhaezrian waters and ports
**Era:** Present Age

## Summary

The Port War is the maritime pressure campaign by pirates armed, tolerated, hired, or cultivated by the [[Continental Empire]]. It has forced ports into restriction, militarization, and emergency supervision.

## Causes

Continental strategy uses deniable maritime forces to weaken island shipping and confidence.

## Course of Events

Pirates struck commerce and coastal movement. The House narrowed trade into protected channels and increased port controls.

## Outcome

[[Kallindra]] bears greater logistical burden and civilian movement is watched more closely.

## Consequences

Tax flows, provisioning, regional trust, and legal norms are all damaged.

- [[Continental Empire]]
- [[House of Béorn]]
