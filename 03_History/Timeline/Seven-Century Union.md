**Date:** c. 10,430–11,130 AHK
**Location:** [[Falhaezrian Isles]]
**Era:** Postwar settlement

## Summary

The [[Seven-Century Union]] followed the first invasion. Freed islands joined [[House of Béorn]] as subjects, creating a deeper archipelagic order.

## Causes

Liberation from the [[Continental Empire]] and the [[High King]]’s demand for tribute and allegiance.

## Course of Events

Administration, taxation, law, port oversight, military duty, and long-distance trade deepened across the archipelago.

## Outcome

The Isles remained united for roughly seven centuries.

## Consequences

The later choice to continue war after the second invasion helped break this unity.

## Contradictions / Open Issues

## Who Benefited

Central state, [[Kallindra]], protected trade.

## Who Lost

Regional autonomy where union felt like subjection.

## Disputed Interpretations

Union, empire, or military protectorate? Depends who is speaking.
