**Date:** c. 900–2800 AHK
**Location:** [[Falhaezrian Isles]]
**Era:** Long Peace

## Summary

The Long Peace was an extended era of internal stability under a long-ruling elven [[High King]]. Trade and inter-island exchange became safer and denser.

## Causes

Institutional hardening after the Age of Willows and reduced internal war.

## Course of Events

The realm normalized councils, offices, obligations, safer exchange, and broader trade.

## Outcome

[[Kallindra]] grew as a major exchange point while [[Rhodokra]] remained a strong inland trade node.

## Consequences

The peace created infrastructure and expectations later damaged by invasion and war.
