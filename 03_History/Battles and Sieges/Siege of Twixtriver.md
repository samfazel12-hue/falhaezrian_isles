**Date:** 2100 BHK
**Location:** [[Twixtriver]] Castle
**Era:** Wars of Sentran Unification

## Summary

[[Béorn]] defeated Krova “Kingkiller” [[Yarrowgold]] at [[Twixtriver]] Castle. Much of the old fortress was destroyed, but Krova escaped aboard the [[Dead Queen]] and later founded the southern kingdom that became [[Yarrowgold]].

## Causes

[[Béorn]]’s unification campaign required breaking Krova, one of the strongest rival warlords.

## Course of Events

[[Béorn]] attacked Twixtriver, destroyed much of the fortress, and forced Krova to flee by ship.

## Outcome

[[Béorn]] removed Krova from [[Sentra]] but failed to erase him as a political threat.

## Consequences

Krova’s southern survival created [[Yarrowgold]] and a long-term counterweight to [[Sentra]].

## Key Figures

- [[Béorn]]
- [[Krova “Kingkiller” Yarrowgold]]

## Contradictions / Open Issues

## Who Benefited

[[Béorn]] in [[Sentra]]; Krova in the sense that he survived to found a new kingdom.

## Who Lost

Twixtriver’s old holders and Krova’s immediate power in [[Sentra]].

## Disputed Interpretations

[[Béornic]] histories call it victory; [[Yarrowgold]] traditions may call it exile, survival, or stolen kingship.
