## Summary

The Battle of Honindra was the turning point of [[Samwise Greensteel Wilgenfal]]’s rise. The ancient holders of Twixtriver were crushed between the [[High House Navy]] and Samwise’s army, and Samwise was proclaimed [[High King]] by the people and part of the Bloodroyal order.

## Causes

Bloodroyal civil war and [[Yarrowgold]] intervention created a decisive military crisis.

## Course of Events

High House naval force and Samwise’s army converged against the enemy. The victory made Samwise the practical solution to the war.

## Outcome

Samwise was proclaimed [[High King]] and moved north against [[House Brandt]].

## Consequences

Honindra made non-Bloodroyal military legitimacy viable at the highest level.
