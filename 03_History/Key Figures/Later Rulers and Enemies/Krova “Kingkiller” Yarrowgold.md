## Overview

Krova was the warlord defeated by [[Béorn]] at Twixtriver. His escape aboard the [[Dead Queen]] led to the southern kingdom that became [[Yarrowgold]].

## Key Actions and Events

- Held Twixtriver before [[Béorn]] drove him out
- Escaped aboard the [[Dead Queen]]
- Carved out a new kingdom on Vesua
