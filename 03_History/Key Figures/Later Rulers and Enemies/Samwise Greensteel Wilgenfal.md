## Overview

Samwise Greensteel Wilgenfal achieved fuller unification of the Isles after defeating the Yellow Army at Honindra. He represents the shift from Bloodroyal founder blood to military and institutional legitimacy.

## Political Position and Allegiances

Aligned with anti-Brandt order, [[House of Béorn]] continuity, and the postwar councils.

## Key Actions and Events

- Defeated the Yellow Army at [[Battle of Honindra]]
- Was proclaimed [[High King]]
- Moved north against [[House Brandt]]
- Founded the Age of Willows / Wilgenfal order
