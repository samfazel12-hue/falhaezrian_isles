## Overview

Berkar is one of the younger Bloodroyal sons and the brutal founder-power behind Blackarrow rule in the eastern [[Kazaros]]. His conquest broke mountain valley polities and left lasting resentment.

## Key Actions and Events

- Founded [[House Blackarrow]]
- Conquered eastern [[Kazaros]] valleys
- Took [[Glacier’s Edge]] at terrible cost
