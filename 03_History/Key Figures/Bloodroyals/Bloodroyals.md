## Notes on Order

Only part of the birth order is fixed: [[Brandt Beornson]] is the eldest son, [[Ella of House Santeber]] is the eldest daughter, [[Berkar Blackarrow]] and [[Eldric of House Aldermark]] are younger sons, and [[Donella]] is the eleventh child. Other sibling order remains open.

The wider tradition also leaves room for unnamed or unresolved Bloodroyal children, especially daughters, if later house lines need them.
