## Overview

Brandt is the eldest Bloodroyal and the ancestor of the Brandt line. His descendants turned eldest-born prestige into a political claim that eventually helped ignite the [[Bloodroyal War]].

## Key Actions and Events

- Remained Brandt Beornson rather than founding a differently named house
- Fathered Sycar, Hadrik, Wolger, and Eckard
- Became symbolic root of the eldest-born claim
