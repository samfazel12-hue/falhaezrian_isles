## Overview

Béorn is the great unifier of [[Sentra]] and the founder figure behind the later [[House of Béorn]]. He moved [[Sentra]] from tribal fragmentation toward sacred kingship and institutional rule.

## Character

Warlord-founder, sacred king, politically ruthless enough to build a lasting order.

## Political Position and Allegiances

Founder of the [[Béornic]] order; allied with and incorporated major Sentran peoples.

## Key Actions and Events

- Unified [[Sentra]] through war, alliance, and incorporation
- Defeated [[Krova “Kingkiller” Yarrowgold]] at [[Siege of Twixtriver]]
- Became King of [[Sentra]] in 2099 BHK
- Sent the [[Bloodroyals]] out from [[Jeor’s Hall]]
