The First Minister is the senior coordinating officer of the [[Second High House Council]] and the leading figure among the ministers of the realm.

## Function

The office coordinates ministries, manages the legislative program, negotiates with the [[First High House Council|First House]], and speaks for the elected ministerial order before the crown.

## Wartime Position

In peace the office can become one of the strongest civil powers in the Isles. In prolonged war it is often overshadowed by the [[High King]].
