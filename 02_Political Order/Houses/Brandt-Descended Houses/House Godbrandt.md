## Overview

House Godbrandt descends from Wolger and took control of [[Meritia]] through political schemes and careful maneuvering. It now defends the [[Augulector Library]] and [[Temple of Heurikissen]] while the [[Meritians]] live displaced.

## Seat and Territory

[[Meritia]], including the [[Augulector Library]] and [[Temple of Heurikissen]].

## Character and Reputation

Learned, sacred-facing, politically slippery, and burdened by the legitimacy problem of controlling another house’s sacred island.
