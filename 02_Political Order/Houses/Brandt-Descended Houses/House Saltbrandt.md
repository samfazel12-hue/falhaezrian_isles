**Founder:** Eckard
**Seat:** [[Saltcliff]]; older [[Rostcliff]] line
**Founded:** c. 900–600 BHK
**Bloodroyal origin:** Bloodroyal-descended; Eckard son of Brandt

## Banner

A small orange and white fox underneath a white tower overlooking an icy sea, on green. The fox memorializes Eckard’s [[Mosswoods]] period with Sycar.

## Overview

House Saltbrandt descends from Eckard and preserves a surviving branch of the wider Brandt inheritance while deliberately keeping the Brandt name.

## Seat and Territory

[[Saltcliff]] over the [[Strait of Brandt]]; [[Rostcliff]] as older associated seat.

## Character and Reputation

Clever, survival-oriented, memorial, and politically careful. Less openly grandiose than the main Brandt claim.

## Dynastic Structure

Eckard first established [[Rostcliff]], then [[Saltcliff]]. [[Rostcliff]] passed to his elder daughter; [[Saltcliff]] went to his son.

## Political Position

Sensitive survivor branch of Brandt descent. Its name choice is a political statement.
