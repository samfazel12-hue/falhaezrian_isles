## Banner

A bearded vulture gliding above a light blue sea, with red mountains on one side and grey mountains on the other.

## Overview

House Redbrandt rose from the Brandt line after opposing the Brandt betrayal. After the [[Bloodroyal War]], it renamed itself House Redrock and set the precedent for post-war name changes.

## Seat and Territory

[[Redcliff]] on [[Ulum]], legally held by The House.

## Character and Reputation

Loyalist, militarized, useful, and politically awkward because its seat sits on [[Ulum]].

## Dynastic Structure

## Political Position

Rewarded for opposing the Brandt betrayal, but still carries Brandt blood and northern tension.
