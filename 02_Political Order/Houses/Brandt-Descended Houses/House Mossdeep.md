**Founder:** Sycar
**Seat:** [[Deepwoodrest]]
**Founded:** c. 900–600 BHK
**Bloodroyal origin:** Bloodroyal-descended; Sycar son of Brandt

## Banner

Variable banner: grey boulder capped with moss, old trees, sometimes forest animals, on brown. The inconsistency is intentional because the [[Mosswoods]] are living and changing.

## Overview

House Mossdeep was founded by Sycar, eldest son of Brandt, after he turned away from Brandt’s stone-and-steel inheritance and bound himself to the [[Mosswoods]] and Anders’s legacy.

## Seat and Territory

[[Deepwoodrest]] and the [[Mosswoods]].

## Character and Reputation

Known for living woodland identity, rejection of rigid Brandt inheritance, and symbolic flexibility.

## Dynastic Structure

Founded by Sycar; tied to Anders though not his direct issue.

## Political Position

Politically useful as a Brandt-descended branch that did not fully follow Brandt ambition.
