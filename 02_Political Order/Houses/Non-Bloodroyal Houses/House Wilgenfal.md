## Banner

Two dark steel swords crossed in front of a silver weeping willow next to a lake with deep blue water, on green.

## Overview

House Wilgenfal is the great non-Bloodroyal house raised by Samwise Greensteel after Honindra and the end of the [[Bloodroyal War]]. It embodies post-Bloodroyal military legitimacy.

## Seat and Territory

Land near the [[Béornic]] heartland and command of [[Stonegate]].

## Character and Reputation

Remembered for steel, willow symbolism, military responsibility, and the shift away from founder blood as sole legitimacy.

## Dynastic Structure

Founded by Samwise; later branch structure.

## Political Position

A pillar of the High Kingdom and House order.
