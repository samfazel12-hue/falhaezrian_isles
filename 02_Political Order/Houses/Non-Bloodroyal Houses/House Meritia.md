## Overview

House [[Meritia]] founded the sacred and scholarly order of [[Meritia]] before losing control to [[House Godbrandt]]. Its political force now lies mostly in displacement and memory.

## Seat and Territory

Island of [[Meritia]] in the [[Great Pearl]].

## Character and Reputation

Sacred, scholarly, dispossessed.

## Dynastic Structure

Founded by Gorman; later displaced after Godbrandt took control of [[Meritia]].

## Political Position

Politically wounded but symbolically important, especially through the Meritian enclave in [[Kallindra]].
