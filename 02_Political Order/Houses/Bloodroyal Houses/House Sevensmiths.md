## Banner

Seven silver hammers arranged like spokes, heads outward, on warm orange deepening to red.

## Overview

House [[Sevensmiths]] grew from Alfrid sheltering refugees made homeless by Berkar’s eastern campaigns. Its authority comes from craft, refuge, and the founding of the great forge.

## Seat and Territory

[[Sevensmiths]] in the [[Kazaros]].

## Character and Reputation

Known for armour, rare metals, and combined smithing traditions from broken mountain clans.

## Dynastic Structure

Alfrid’s confirmed children are Torman, Esthra, Lillia, and Derk.

## Political Position

House-aligned but morally contrasted with Berkar’s brutality.
