## Overview

House Brandt descends from [[Brandt Beornson]], eldest child of [[Béorn]] and [[Rosaria]]. It became the ideological center of eldest-born right and the great defeated line of the [[Bloodroyal War]].

## Seat and Territory

Originally tied to [[Skystone]] and the eldest Bloodroyal inheritance; later stripped, exiled, and scattered.

## Character and Reputation

Prestigious, ambitious, dangerous, and remembered as the line that tried to turn founder seniority into ruling principle.

## Dynastic Structure

Confirmed sons include Sycar, Hadrik, Wolger, and Eckard. Later branches include Mossdeep, Godbrandt, Saltbrandt, Redbrandt/Redrock.

## Political Position

Defeated and exiled. Surviving branches remain politically sensitive.
