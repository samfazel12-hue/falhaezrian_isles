**Founder:** [[Berkar Blackarrow]]
**Seat:** [[Arrowpoint]]
**Founded:** Founder Age
**Bloodroyal origin:** yes; Berkar, younger Bloodroyal son

## Banner

A black arrow with red notched fletching pointed downward over a pale grey mountain on stone-grey.

## Overview

House Blackarrow descends from Berkar and holds power through mountain passes, valley polities, and the eastern [[Kazaros]] conquest.

## Seat and Territory

[[Arrowpoint]] and associated eastern [[Kazaros]] holdings; cadet branch holds [[Bloodhorn]].

## Character and Reputation

Militarized, ambitious, mountain-hard, and shadowed by Berkar’s brutal campaigns.

## Dynastic Structure

Berkar’s includes Falik, Karn, Arrek, Dorrek, Yrsa, and Maraeka.

## Political Position

Likely aligned with the House but carries internal mountain resentments from conquest.
