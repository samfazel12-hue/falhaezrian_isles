## Overview

Clawpoint is a small fort, watchtower, and lighthouse on [[Amberhorn]]. It guides ships toward [[Featherport]] and the [[Port of the People]].

## Physical Description

Coastal tower and lighthouse installation.

## Strategic Function

Navigation aid, watch post, and warning station for golden-hulled [[Yarrowgold]] ships.

## History

Recently also functions as a wildlife reserve with marine biologists.

## Controlled Routes

approaches to [[Featherport]] and [[Port of the People]]

## Garrison and Supplies

Small watch staff; possibly reduced military role in peacetime.

## Vulnerabilities

Small size and mixed conservation role.

## Political Importance

Shows how defense, science, and maritime traffic overlap in the Shields.
