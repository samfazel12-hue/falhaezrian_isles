**Region:** [[Ulum and the Red Sea]]
**Type:** House enclave / fortress settlement
**Status:** active

## Overview

Redcliff sits on [[Ulum]] but legally belongs to The House. It is a practical settlement and a constant low-grade irritation in House-Ulum relations.

## Geography and Access

On [[Ulum]], facing the Red Sea and the mountain/coastal dwarf world.

## Economy and Function

Fortified House presence, port or coastal control, military administration.

## Politics and Power

House property inside dwarven territory.
