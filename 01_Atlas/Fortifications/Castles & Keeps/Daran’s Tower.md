## Overview

Daran’s Tower is the palace of [[High King]] [[Daran I Redrock]] on [[King's Rest]]. It is a palace enclosed by a fortress.

## Physical Description

Tower, palace, and extremely thick walls built by Daran I and [[Daran II]]. Wall thickness ranges from 12 to 30 metres.

## Strategic Function

Royal residence and defensible island stronghold.

## History

Daran I built the tower and palace; [[Daran II]] built the walls.

## Controlled Routes

[[King's Rest]] maritime approaches

## Garrison and Supplies

Castle staff, royal guard, island supply.

## Vulnerabilities

Kitchens and stables sit in the most vulnerable positions inside the walls.

## Political Importance

Material expression of Redrock royal authority.
