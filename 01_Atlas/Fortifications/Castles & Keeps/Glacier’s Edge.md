**Held by:** [[House Glacirbar]] survivor line / Blackarrow-imposed line
**Part of:** Kazarosian castles
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Glacier’s Edge was the last castle to fall to Berkar’s conquest of the eastern [[Kazaros]]. Its fall cost so much that it effectively ended the campaign.

## Physical Description

Relatively small castle close enough to glacial water to endure siege.

## Strategic Function

Controlled a hard mountain position with secure water.

## History

Berkar lost two sons and thousands of soldiers here. He exterminated nearly the whole ruling house, spared one daughter, and married her into his line.

## Controlled Routes

eastern [[Kazaros]] approaches

## Garrison and Supplies

Small but stubborn mountain defense.

## Vulnerabilities

Could be isolated, but its water source made starvation difficult.

## Political Importance

A permanent stain on Blackarrow legitimacy.
