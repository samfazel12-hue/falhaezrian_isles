**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** waycastle chain
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Silvercloud is the last major waycastle before [[Skystone]] and is almost invisible from outside.

## Physical Description

Gates and portcullis sit flush with the cliff face. Interior is long and narrow, running through the mountain. The far side opens onto the northern face and the view of [[Kazaros]] and [[Ulum]].

## Strategic Function

Final defensive filter before [[Skystone]]; controls mountain-through passage.
