**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]] / [[Donella]] legacy
**Part of:** waycastle chain
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Rockgarden is the most unusual waycastle: a living fortress-arboretum built into a levelled mountainside.

## Physical Description

Green copper and glass, domed ends, half-buried into the slope, with terraced stone, streams, waterfalls, vines, moss, floating plants, and a public arboretum at its face.

## Strategic Function

Forces all ascent traffic through the castle. There is no path around without serious climbing gear. Also preserves [[Kazaros]] biodiversity and [[Donella]]’s legacy.

## History

Original seat of [[Donella]], eleventh child of [[Béorn]] and [[Rosaria]]. Now partly public through the arboretum and biology faculty.
