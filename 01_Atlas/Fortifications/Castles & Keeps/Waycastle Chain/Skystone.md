**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** waycastle chain / crown redoubt
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Skystone is the seat of the [[High King]] and the most heavily defended position in the realm. It sits at the peak of the [[Kazaros]] waycastle chain and has never fallen.

## Physical Description

A summit fortress reached only after passing through the ascending waycastle chain. The final approach is deliberately narrow enough for controlled carts, supplies, and defenders.

## Strategic Function

Ultimate royal redoubt; protects the crown, archives, command, and continuity of the House.

## History

Originally the ancestral seat of [[House Brandt]]. Taken by the House after the [[Bloodroyal War]], turning the eldest line’s stronghold into the crown’s citadel.
