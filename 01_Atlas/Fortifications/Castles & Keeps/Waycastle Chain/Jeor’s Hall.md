**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** waycastle chain / sacred founder site
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Jeor’s Hall is the old hall-fort beneath [[Kacore]], built by [[Béorn]]’s father. It is the symbolic starting point of the Bloodroyal founder order and the ascent to [[Skystone]].

## Physical Description

An old fortified hall at the mountain’s foot, more ancestral and sacred than purely military.

## Strategic Function

Controls the beginning of the [[Skystone]] ascent and anchors [[Béornic]] founder legitimacy.

## History

[[Béorn]] was born and raised here. The [[Bloodroyals]] were sent out from here to found houses, seats, domains, and institutions.
