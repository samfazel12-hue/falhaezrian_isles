**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** [[Waycastle chain]]
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Darèh Gap is the smallest waycastle, but its terrain makes it decisive. Anyone advancing must cross its bridge.

## Physical Description

Two towers connected by a narrow bridge over a three-hundred-metre drop into Darèh Rift. Barely a castle in scale, but enormous in tactical value.

## Strategic Function

Controls the only practical crossing through this point of the ascent.

## History

Built into the defensive logic of the waycastle chain.

## Controlled Routes

Bridge over Darèh Rift.

## Garrison and Supplies

Small garrison, high discipline, enough supplies to hold a choke point.

## Vulnerabilities

If attackers control both sides or destroy the bridge, the route is broken for everyone.

## Political Importance

A minimal fortification with maximal strategic effect.
