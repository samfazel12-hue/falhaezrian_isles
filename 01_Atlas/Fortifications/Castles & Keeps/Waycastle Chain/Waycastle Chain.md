**Region:** [[Eastern Sentra]]
**Part of:** [[Waycastle Chain]]

## Overview

The defended mountain corridor from [[Jeor’s Hall]] to [[Skystone]]. Any force threatening the crown from below must fight upward through the chain.

## Strategic Function

The chain is not decorative. It explains why the eastern core and the [[High King]]’s seat are so hard to take.
