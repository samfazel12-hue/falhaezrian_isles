**Held by:** local Kazarosian holders
**Part of:** Kazarosian castles
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Meltwatch guards a small valley and draws water directly from a glacier. Its name is literal: it watches the melt and the pass.

## Physical Description

Keep near a glacier descending close to its walls.

## Strategic Function

Pass watch, valley control, water-secure defense.

## History

Resisted Berkar’s conquest because its position and water supply let it outlast pressure.

## Controlled Routes

small [[Kazaros]] valley route

## Garrison and Supplies

Valley garrison with reliable water.

## Vulnerabilities

Dependent on glacier access and narrow local geography.

## Political Importance

Useful symbol of mountain resistance.
