## Overview

Rostcliff is the older seat of Eckard Saltbrandt’s line. Eckard settled there before taking [[Saltcliff]].

## Physical Description

Small Kazarosian castle.

## Strategic Function

Older inland seat for the Saltbrandt branch.

## History

When Eckard moved to [[Saltcliff]], Rostcliff passed to his elder daughter.

## Controlled Routes

[[Kazaros]] routes between Saltbrandt holdings

## Garrison and Supplies

House retinue rather than major army.

## Vulnerabilities

Less important than [[Saltcliff]], therefore easier to neglect.

## Political Importance

Matters for inheritance and older Saltbrandt legitimacy.
