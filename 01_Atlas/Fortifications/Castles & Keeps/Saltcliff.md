**Region:** [[Ulum and the Red Sea]]
**Type:** castle settlement
**Status:** active

## Overview

Saltcliff is the seat of [[House Saltbrandt]] and stands over the [[Strait of Brandt]], a key northern choke point between [[Sentra]] and [[Ulum]].

## Geography and Access

Overlooking the [[Strait of Brandt]].

## Economy and Function

Strait control, military supply, coastal authority.

## Politics and Power

Saltbrandt seat with Brandt-descended memory.
