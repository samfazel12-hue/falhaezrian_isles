**Held by:** [[Yarrowgold]] or [[Sentra]] depending current control
**Part of:** naval base fortification
**Region:** [[Surgemark]]

## Overview

Wrackholm is the castle and primary fortification on [[Surgemark]], built for naval base function rather than noble display.

## Physical Description

Working military installation organized around the harbour it commands.

## Strategic Function

Controls [[Surgemark]]’s naval base and the southern approach to [[Sentra]] through [[Surgewater Gulf]].

## History

Changed hands repeatedly along with [[Surgemark]].

## Controlled Routes

Harbour and southern approach sea lanes.

## Garrison and Supplies

Naval garrison, repair stores, harbour defenses.

## Vulnerabilities

Dependent on [[Archvikport]] and sea supply; exposed to blockade.

## Political Importance

Whoever holds Wrackholm threatens southern [[Sentra]] and Kallindran harbours.
