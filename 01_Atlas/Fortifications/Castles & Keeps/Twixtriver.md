## Overview

Twixtriver is a fortress-site and river crossing in [[Sentra]], best known for the [[Siege of Twixtriver]].

[[Twixtriver]] was held by [[Gartun Rostmodya]] before its seizure in the name of [[Yarrawe]]. Gartun and his kin were put to the sword. The site later became the battlefield where [[Béorn]] defeated [[Krova Kingkiller Yarrowgold]].

## Geography

Twixtriver controls a river crossing between inland [[Sentra]], southern routes, and the Yarrowgold-aligned south.

Known elements:

- [[Twixtriver]]
- the river crossing
- nearby road and river traffic
- the old siege field

## History

[[Gartun Rostmodya]] and his kin held [[Twixtriver]] before the Yarrawe seizure.

The castle was taken in the name of [[Yarrawe]], goddess over [[Yarrowgold]]. Gartun's line was destroyed or broken there.

During the [[Wars of Sentran Unification]], Twixtriver became the site of Béorn's victory over [[Krova Kingkiller Yarrowgold]].

## Siege of Twixtriver

- **Date:** [[2100 BHK]]
- **Conflict:** [[Wars of Sentran Unification]]
- **Victor:** [[Béorn]]
- **Defeated power:** [[Krova Kingkiller Yarrowgold]]
- **Site:** [[Twixtriver]]
- **Result:** Krova defeated; Béorn proclaimed King of Sentra in [[2099 BHK]]

## Political Memory

Twixtriver is remembered through several layers: Rostmodya dead, Yarrawe claim, Yarrowgold defeat, Béornic victory, and local versions of the castle's fall.
