## Overview

Twixtriver is a fortress-site and river crossing in [[Sentra]], best known for the [[Siege of Twixtriver]] and the fall of [[Gartun Rostmodya]].

Before its capture, Twixtriver Castle was held by Gartun Rostmodya and his kin. They were put to the sword, and the castle was taken in the name of [[Yarrawe]], goddess over [[Yarrowgold]].

The site became one of the key founder-era battlegrounds in the struggle between [[Béorn]] and the Yarrowgold-aligned powers of the south.

## Political Status

Twixtriver’s early history is tied to local rule by [[Gartun Rostmodya]].

The seizure of the castle in the name of [[Yarrawe]] made Twixtriver part of the Yarrowgold religious-political sphere before Béorn’s final victory. This matters because the castle was not only taken militarily. It was claimed through divine authority.

Later House histories remember Twixtriver as the decisive site where Béorn defeated [[Krova Kingkiller Yarrowgold]] and completed the unification of Sentra.

## The Taking in Yarrawe’s Name

Twixtriver Castle was taken in the name of [[Yarrawe]], goddess over [[Yarrowgold]].

Gartun Rostmodya and his kin were put to the sword. The act likely carried both military and sacred meaning: the old holders were removed, the castle was purified or claimed, and the site was folded into Yarrowgold’s divine-political order.
