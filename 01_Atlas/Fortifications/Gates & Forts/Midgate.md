**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** [[Moonhills]] passage gate
**Region:** [[Moonhills]]

## Overview

Midgate is the main passage between eastern and western [[Sentra]] and the only point where the [[Redstone Road]] runs east-west through the [[Moonhills]].

## Physical Description

Tall white curving walls climb the fog-covered valley slopes on both sides, following terrain rather than merely blocking the road.

## Strategic Function

Controls the primary legal and reliable passage through the [[Moonhills]].

## History

Its existence helped shape the limited contact between west and east.

## Controlled Routes

[[Redstone Road]] through the [[Moonhills]].

## Garrison and Supplies

Permanent gate garrison; likely inspectors due to western tensions.

## Vulnerabilities

Fog, slopes, and hidden paths complicate control beyond the walls.

## Political Importance

Midgate is a border instrument as much as a fortification.
