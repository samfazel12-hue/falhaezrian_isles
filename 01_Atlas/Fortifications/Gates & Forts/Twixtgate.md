**Held by:** [[Kallindra]]
**Part of:** city gate
**Region:** [[Kallindra]]

## Overview

Twixtgate spans the [[Twixt]] river and connects both banks of [[Kallindra]].

## Physical Description

Gate over water rather than only through a wall.

## Strategic Function

Controls river crossing inside the city.

## History

Named from its position over the [[Twixt]].

## Controlled Routes

[[Twixt]] river crossing

## Garrison and Supplies

City gate guard.

## Vulnerabilities

Bridge-gates are vulnerable to sabotage and river traffic disruption.

## Political Importance

Controls movement between urban halves.
