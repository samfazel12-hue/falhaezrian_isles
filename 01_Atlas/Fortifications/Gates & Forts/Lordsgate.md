**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** border fort / military staging camp
**Region:** Central/[[Southern Sentra]]

## Overview

Lordsgate is an ancient fort on the border between the [[Béornic]] heartland and [[Ignahuatl]] south. It has shifted from internal boundary marker to wartime staging camp.

## Physical Description

Old fort with a small settlement around it. Much sits empty in peacetime, but wartime fills it with soldiers and training grounds.

## Strategic Function

Originally marked and enforced the boundary between two peoples; now projects soldiers outward toward the Continental war.

## History

Accumulated settlement over time. Its south-facing walls preserve the memory of older competition.

## Controlled Routes

Roads between [[Béornic]] heartland and [[Ignahuatl]] south; troop routes toward ports.

## Garrison and Supplies

Modest peacetime garrison; wartime thousands of soldiers.

## Vulnerabilities

Empty peacetime spaces can hide decay, corruption, or illicit use.

## Political Importance

A physical reminder that current unity sits on older internal frontiers.
