**Held by:** [[House Blackarrow]]
**Part of:** checkpoint
**Region:** [[Kazaros]]

## Overview

Fletcher’s Gate is the checkpoint before [[Arrowpoint]]. It is not a grand fortress; it is a filter.

## Physical Description

Small fortified gate with permanent garrison.

## Strategic Function

Controls all road traffic approaching [[Arrowpoint]].

## History

Developed as part of [[Arrowpoint]]’s defensive-administrative system.

## Controlled Routes

road to [[Arrowpoint]]

## Garrison and Supplies

Small permanent garrison.

## Vulnerabilities

Too small to resist a serious army alone.

## Political Importance

Lets Blackarrow inspect, tax, delay, or deny movement.
