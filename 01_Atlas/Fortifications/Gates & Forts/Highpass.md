**Held by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** [[Moonhills]] fortress
**Region:** [[Moonhills]]

## Overview

Highpass is the only other clear route through the [[Moonhills]] besides [[Midgate]], reached by a game trail.

## Physical Description

Large fortress in difficult hill terrain. More isolated and less trafficked than [[Midgate]].

## Strategic Function

Controls the alternate passage through the hills and prevents western-east movement from avoiding [[Midgate]] entirely.

## History

Maintained because one gate cannot control the whole [[Moonhills]].

## Controlled Routes

Game trail passage through [[Moonhills]].

## Garrison and Supplies

Garrison suited to bad weather, patrols, and low traffic.

## Vulnerabilities

Hard to supply; hidden paths may exist nearby.

## Political Importance

Important precisely because it is the route people use when they cannot or should not use [[Midgate]].
