**Held by:** [[House Wilgenfal]] / [[House of Béorn]]
**Part of:** eastern defense / major gate
**Region:** [[Eastern Sentra]]

## Overview

Stonegate is the harsher and narrower of the great eastern gate systems. It is one of the greatest strategic responsibilities in [[Sentra]].

## Physical Description

A major fortress controlling a tight route through steep ground where movement is forced into one approach. Earlier concept: massive stone gate raised by metal chains.

## Strategic Function

Controls the mountain route into the western woods and blocks major land movement.

## History

Granted to [[House Wilgenfal]] after Samwise’s rise. Remained a western lock of the High Kingdom.

## Controlled Routes

Main mountain route into western woods.

## Garrison and Supplies

Large garrisons, troops trained for mountain war, heavy supply obligations.

## Vulnerabilities

If bypassed through tunnels or betrayal, its strength becomes political theatre.

## Political Importance

Command of Stonegate legitimizes [[House Wilgenfal]] as a military pillar of the post-Bloodroyal order.
