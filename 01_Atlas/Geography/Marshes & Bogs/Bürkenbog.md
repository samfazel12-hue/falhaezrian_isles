**Region:** [[Sentra]]
**Type:** Marsh / bog

## Overview

A bog on the western and southern edge of the [[Great Pearl]], reaching toward the [[Saronago]]. It is named for its half-drowned dark birch trees.

## Movement

This terrain is difficult to cross in force. Armies, smugglers, hunters, poisoners, patrols, and nearby settlements all have to account for it.

## Resources and Hazards

- Wet ground
- Poor roads
- Local wildlife
- Concealment
- Disease or poison potential
