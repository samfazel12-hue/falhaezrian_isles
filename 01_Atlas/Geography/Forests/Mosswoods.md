**Parent region:** [[Central Sentra]]
**Controlled by:** [[House Mossdeep]] / local woodland powers
**Primary terrain:** ancient temperate rainforest remnant

## Overview

The Mosswoods are a moss-heavy woodland of hills, streams, and old growth. They matter because Anders built [[Deepwoodrest]] there and Sycar later rooted [[House Mossdeep]] in its living character.

## Geography

Hilly forest cut by streams and fed by Moonhill drainage.

## People and Culture

Woodland communities and Mossdeep retainers shaped by isolation, forest law, and old house memory.

## Political Status

Tied to [[House Mossdeep]] and the Brandt-descended break from eldest-line ambition.

## Settlements

[[Deepwoodrest]]

## Key Geography

- Rivers: [[Let]]
- Seats: [[Deepwoodrest]]

## History

Part of the ancient [[Patahyrcazar]] rainforest before the [[Dragon War]] broke the wider canopy.

## Routes and Barriers

Difficult forest movement; not a clean military highway.

## Strategic Value

The forest gives cover, identity, and defensive depth to Mossdeep.

## Resources

Timber, herbs, mosses, forest craft.
