**Parent region:** [[Southern Sentra]]
**Controlled by:** Sentran / [[Ignahuatl]] border influence
**Primary terrain:** vitrified forest and glass plains

## Overview

The Broken Forest is the [[Dragon War]] wound that never healed. Dragonfire vitrified parts of the soil into glass plains, leaving a broken canopy that still cannot fully recover.

## Geography

Regrown woodland interrupted by dark reflective glass clearings. Some patches are field-sized; others run for a quarter mile or more.

## People and Culture

[[Ignahuatl]] memory treats the edge of the forest as a sacred trauma. [[Culverno]] craftsmen harvest serpentglass from the glass plains.

## Political Status

The material economy pulls the forest toward [[Culverno]], while the wider region remains under Sentran pressure.

## Settlements

[[Last Stand]], [[Culverno]]

## Key Geography

- Glass plains
- Edge settlement: [[Last Stand]]

## History

A dragon destroyed settlements, holds, and castles here during the [[Dragon War]] about eight thousand years ago.

## Routes and Barriers

Dangerous footing, heat-retaining glass, poor water absorption, broken cover.

## Strategic Value

Controls the only known source of serpentglass.

## Resources

[[Serpentglass]].
