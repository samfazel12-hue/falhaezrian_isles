## Overview

Hyrcasos is a surviving fragment of the ancient [[Patahyrcazar]] rainforest and gives [[Hyrcsis]] its name. It is one of the ecological survivors of the [[Dragon War]]’s long damage.

## Geography

Temperate forest fragment near the breadbasket and Moonhill-fed rivers.

## People and Culture

Nearby agricultural communities treat it as a named landscape rather than empty wilderness.

## Political Status

No fixed ruler yet beyond surrounding Sentran authority.

## Settlements

[[Hyrcsis]]

## Key Geography

- Forests: Hyrcasos
- Related woods: [[Mosswoods]], [[Noltic Woods]], [[Broken Forest]]

## History

Once part of a single vast rainforest with [[Mosswoods]] and [[Noltic Woods]].

Woodland products, old-growth pockets, local identity.
