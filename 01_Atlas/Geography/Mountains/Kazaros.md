## Overview

The Kazaros is a broad mountainous plateau in eastern and northern [[Sentra]]. It runs northwest to southeast across the island and contains high plateaus, worn ridges, deep valleys, glacial water, hidden passes, old mountain settlements, and fortified roads.

The range is the core of eastern House power. It contains [[Kacore]], [[Skystone]], the [[Waycastle Chain]], the [[Kazaros Tunnel Network]], major forge settlements, and several castles tied to Bloodroyal and post-Bloodroyal house politics.

The Kazaros is not only a barrier. It is a settled region with its own houses, valleys, routes, grudges, and older mountain identities.

## Geography

The Kazaros is geologically old and worn, but still high enough to hold snow in its upper reaches for much of the year due to continental lift. Its terrain includes high ridges, broad elevated ground, deep-cut valleys, cliffs, rifts, glacial streams, narrow roads, caves, and hidden internal routes.

The range is wide enough that movement inside it depends on local knowledge. A road, tunnel, bridge, valley, or pass can decide military control more than distance alone.

## Tunnel Network

The [[Kazaros Tunnel Network]] connects castles and keeps of the eastern mountain chain and links through deeper routes toward [[Ulum]].

The surface dwarves of [[Ulum]] taught the tunneling knowledge that made the system possible. The internal Kazaros tunnels were built by the House and are not fully known to the Ulumi dwarves. The larger connecting routes between Sentra and Ulum are known to both sides, while both powers may know passages the other does not.

The network explains why the eastern core has never truly fallen. Surface roads, ports, and passes can be cut, while [[Skystone]] can still receive supplies and communication through the mountains.

## Military Role

The Kazaros protects the eastern core of Sentra and the survival of the crown.

Its defensive system includes:

- the [[Waycastle Chain]]
- [[Skystone]]
- the [[Kazaros Tunnel Network]]
- fortified passes
- valley castles
- narrow roads
- local mountain garrisons
- forge settlements
- House-aligned mountain houses
- difficult winter conditions
- limited approach routes

The range does not need to stop every army at its edge. It slows, divides, starves, filters, and exhausts forces before they reach the crown.

## History

The Kazaros held mountain kingdoms and valley polities before [[Béorn]] unified Sentra.

After unification, the range became the eastern fortress-core of the House. [[Jeor’s Hall]] preserved founder memory beneath [[Kacore]], while [[Skystone]] became the supreme political and military seat.

[[Skystone]] was originally the ancestral seat of [[House Brandt]]. After the [[Bloodroyal War]], it passed into the crown’s control and became the symbol of Brandt defeat and royal survival.

[[Berkar Blackarrow]]’s campaigns reshaped the eastern Kazaros. His conquests broke mountain clans and valley houses. [[Glacier’s Edge]] was the last castle to fall, after a siege that cost Berkar two sons and thousands of soldiers. The cost effectively ended his campaign.

[[Alfrid the Forgeborn]] later sheltered refugees from Berkar’s campaigns. The six master smiths who came to him became part of the founding memory of [[Sevensmiths]].
