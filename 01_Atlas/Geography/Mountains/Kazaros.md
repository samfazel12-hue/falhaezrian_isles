**Parent region:** [[Sentra]]
**Controlled by:** [[House of Béorn]], mountain houses, local valley holders
**Primary terrain:** old broad mountain range; high plateaus, ridges, valleys, cliffs, glaciers
**Political role:** crown redoubt / eastern mountain heartland

## Overview

The Kazaros is the broad mountain plateau of eastern and northern [[Sentra]].

The Kazaros is not only a barrier. It is a settled region with roads, tunnels, valleys, castles, smiths, snowmelt, sacred memory, and political scars.

## Geography

The range is old, broad, and worn, but high enough to hold snow in its upper reaches. Movement depends on local knowledge: a road, tunnel, bridge, side pass, valley hold, or winter crossing can matter more than distance.

Important features include [[Kacore]], Darèh Rift, [[Bloody Pass]], the eastern Kazaros, the northern Ulum-facing frontier, glacial valleys, and the Skystone summit.

## Settlements and Forts

Major sites include [[Skystone]], [[Jeor’s Hall]], [[Rockgarden]], [[Sevensmiths]], [[Arrowpoint]], [[Torhin]], [[Zarienel]], [[Rostcliff]], [[Meltwatch]], [[Glacier’s Edge]], [[Bloodhorn]], [[Fletcher’s Gate]], [[Darèh Gap]], and [[Silvercloud]].

The region's politics are split between the crown, [[House Blackarrow]], [[House Sevensmiths]], remnants or memories of [[House Glacirbar]], [[House Saltbrandt]] branches, local Kazarosian holders, and [[Ulum]] influence in the north.

## Tunnel Network

Surface dwarves of Ulum taught the tunneling knowledge that made the system possible. The internal Kazaros tunnels were built by the House and are not fully known to the Ulumi dwarves. The larger connecting routes are known to both sides, while each power may know passages the other does not.

The network explains why the eastern core has never truly fallen. Roads and passes can be cut while Skystone still receives supplies and messages through the mountains.

## Military Role

The Kazaros protects the crown by slowing, dividing, starving, filtering, and exhausting attackers before they reach [[Skystone]].

Its defense rests on the waycastles, tunnels, passes, valley castles, narrow roads, winter weather, forge settlements, local garrisons, and mountain houses. It does not need to stop every army at the edge. It needs to make approach costly and uncertain.

## History

The Kazaros held mountain kingdoms and valley polities before Béornic unification.

After unification, it became the eastern fortress-core of the House. [[Jeor’s Hall]] preserved founder memory beneath [[Kacore]], while [[Skystone]] became the supreme political and military seat.

Skystone was originally tied to [[House Brandt]]. After the [[Bloodroyal War]], it passed into crown control and became a symbol of Brandt defeat and royal survival.

[[Berkar Blackarrow]] reshaped the eastern Kazaros. His campaigns broke mountain clans and valley houses. [[Glacier’s Edge]] was the last castle to fall, after a siege that cost Berkar two sons and thousands of soldiers.

[[Alfrid the Forgeborn]] later sheltered refugees from Berkar's campaigns. The six master smiths who came to him became part of the founding memory of [[Sevensmiths]].

## Economy

The Kazaros provides metals, stone, glacial water, armscraft, tunnels, mountain timber, road control, and forge skill. [[Sevensmiths]], [[Torhin]], and smaller forges make the range essential to war supply.
