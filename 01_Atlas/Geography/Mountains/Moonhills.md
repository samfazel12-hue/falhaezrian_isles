**Parent region:** [[Sentra]]
**Controlled by:** [[House of Béorn]] at gates; locally contested by geography
**Primary terrain:** foggy karst hills

## Overview

The Moonhills form the main barrier between western [[Sentra]] and the [[Béornic]] heartland. Their role is less height than difficulty: fog, rain, karst, thorn growth, floods, and unreliable paths.

## Geography

Low rolling hills with unstable karst geology, sinkholes, cave collapses, hollow ground, thorny valleys, flash-flooding rivers, winter fog, and heavy rain where fronts collide.

## People and Culture

The hills helped preserve western cultural distinctiveness by restricting movement. Communities near the passes live with constant military and weather discipline.

## Political Status

Controlled through gates rather than through total occupation. The terrain itself does much of the work.
