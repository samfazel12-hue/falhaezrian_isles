**Region:** [[Sentra]]
**Type:** Lake

## Overview

A smaller eastern lake below the eastern heights. Its name frames it as the lesser counterpart to the [[Great Pearl]], serving a narrower basin and a smaller ring of settlements.

## Geography and Access

The lake functions as a local movement and settlement anchor, with roads, ferries, and small landing places gathered around it.

## Strategic Value

Control of the lake matters where it supports trade, food movement, troop movement, or regional identity.
