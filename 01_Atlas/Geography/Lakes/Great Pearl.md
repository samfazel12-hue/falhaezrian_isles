**Region:** [[Sentra]]
**Type:** Lake

## Overview

The largest inland water of [[Sentra]] and the core of a major basin. It receives the [[Norgon]] and [[Sargon]] and drains south through the [[Saronago]]. It links the interior to trade routes running toward [[Kallindra]].

## Geography and Access

The lake functions as a local movement and settlement anchor, with roads, ports, ferries, and river routes gathered around its shores.

## Strategic Value

Control of the lake matters where it supports trade, food movement, troop movement, or regional identity.
