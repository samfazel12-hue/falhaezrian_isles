## Overview

The Great Pearl Basin is the inland water and river system centered on the [[Great Pearl]].

It receives water from the [[Norgon]] and [[Sargon]], connects to the [[Let]] and [[Erkzis]] through central Sentran drainage, and drains south through the [[Saronago]] toward [[Kallindra]] and the sea.

The basin is one of the main logistical systems of [[Sentra]]. Grain, timber, metals, passengers, troops, taxes, ferries, fish, river craft, lake cargo, and secrets move through it.

## Geography

The basin is built around the [[Great Pearl]], its feeder rivers, its bog margins, and the towns that control movement around the water.

The region includes fertile floodplains, river confluences, lake ports, ferry landings, fishing grounds, storage towns, old lake settlements, timber routes, grain routes, and wetland barriers.

## Political Status

The Great Pearl Basin is formally under [[House of Béorn]], but power is divided.

The crown needs the basin for food, transit, military supply, and tax movement. Local towns control the practical machinery. [[Rhodokra]] has older urban autonomy and commercial reach. [[Bastel]] controls inland flow. [[Bearhaven]] handles lake-port transfer. [[Meritia]] remains politically sensitive because of sacred institutions, displacement, and [[House Godbrandt]] control.

## History

The basin was settled before [[Béornic]] unification. [[Rhodokra]] began as a small lake-town of wooden huts, landing places, and a temple before becoming a major trade hub.

After unification, the basin became one of the main inland supply systems of the House order. Its rivers and lake routes linked central agriculture, northern timber, mountain goods, sacred institutions, and Kallindran trade.

[[Meritia]] became a major island centre of scholarship and worship through [[House Meritia]], the [[Augulector Library]], and the [[Temple of Heurikissen]]. Later control by [[House Godbrandt]] made the island politically sensitive.

The present war has increased crown pressure on grain, boats, warehouses, river tolls, and lake ports.

## Strategic Value

The basin feeds and moves central Sentra.

Its value comes from:

- inland grain movement
- lake transport
- river traffic
- transfer toward [[Kallindra]]
- access to [[Rhodokra]]
- [[Meritia]]’s sacred and scholarly institutions
- storage and inspection sites
- road-water integration
- control over ferries, bridges, and ports
- ability to move troops and supplies without relying only on coastal roads
