## Overview

Lakewatch is the smallest surviving fragment of the [[Patahyrcazar]] rainforest. Its people preserve the forest through magic rather than outside aid.

## Geography and Access

Small isolated forest remnant near the lake.

## Economy and Function

Limited local subsistence; magical forest tending matters more than trade.

## Politics and Power

Lakewatchers keep out of wider Sentran politics.
