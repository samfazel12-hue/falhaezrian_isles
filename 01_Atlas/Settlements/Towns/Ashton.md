## Overview

Ashton is a lumber milling town in the [[Sentran Breadbasket]]. It sits between the [[Norgon]] and [[Sargon]] floodplains, where fertile soil and river access make it useful without making it politically central.

## Geography and Access

Between the floodplains of the [[Norgon]] and [[Sargon]].

## Economy and Function

Lumber milling, river movement, agricultural support.
