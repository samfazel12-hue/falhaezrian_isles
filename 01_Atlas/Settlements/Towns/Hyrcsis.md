## Overview

Hyrcsis is a prosperous agricultural settlement on a rise above the dangerous [[Erkzis]] flood zone. It is useful, stable, and local rather than grand.

## Geography and Access

Set back from the [[Erkzis]] on higher ground, surrounded by plains, rolling hills, and vineyards.

## Economy and Function

Agriculture, vineyards, breadbasket distribution.

## Politics and Power

Ordinary Sentran local rule; not a major political node.
