**Region:** [[Béornic]] Heartland
**Type:** militarized settlement
**Status:** active

## Overview

Torhin is a northern frontier-support town with heavy [[Ulum]] influence. It is one of the clearest contact points between Sentran and dwarven methods.

## Geography and Access

Northern [[Sentra]] near the Ulum-facing frontier.

## Economy and Function

Military support, frontier supply, dwarven-influenced craft.

## Politics and Power

House authority mixed with [[Ulum]] habits and materials.
