## Overview

Bastel is the largest town in central [[Sentra]] and the main inland distribution point of the breadbasket. Its importance is transit importance: grain and goods pass through it before moving toward the [[Great Pearl]] and [[Kallindra]].

## Geography and Access

It sits at the confluence of the [[Norgon]], [[Let]], and [[Erkzis]]. Raft routes and river roads connect it to the lake system.

## Economy and Function

Grain, farm goods, and inland merchandise pass through Bastel. It is prosperous but not glamorous.

## Politics and Power

Economically important but politically overshadowed by [[Rhodokra]] and [[Torhin]].
