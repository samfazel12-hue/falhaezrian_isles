**Region:** [[Great Pearl Basin]]
**Type:** port
**Status:** active

## Overview

Bearhaven is a port and distribution centre on the shores of the [[Great Pearl]]. It handles goods moving between the eastern heartland and [[Kallindra]].

## Geography and Access

Located on the [[Great Pearl]] with access to internal water routes.

## Economy and Function

Portage, storage, inspection, lake transport, and redistribution.

## Politics and Power

Likely under House-supervised civil port administration during wartime.
