## Overview

Last Stand is both a settlement and a battlefield. It marks the place where the [[Ignahuatl]] stopped retreating from the dragon that destroyed the forest behind them.

## Geography and Access

At the edge of the [[Broken Forest]], with glass plains behind the old defensive line.

## Economy and Function

Memory, pilgrimage, local trade, battlefield custodianship.

## Politics and Power

Culturally [[Ignahuatl]]. Its legitimacy comes from remembrance rather than administrative rank.
