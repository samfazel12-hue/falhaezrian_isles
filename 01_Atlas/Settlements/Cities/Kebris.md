## Overview

Kebris is the largest city in the [[Sentran Shields]] and the main urban center of [[Larhorak]]. It was originally a city-state, but has since been fully absorbed into wider Falhaezrian politics.

The city matters because it is the clearest example of a shield-island civic power losing independent status without necessarily losing local pride. It should not feel like a minor port. It is the largest city in its island chain and probably still sees itself as more than a provincial outpost.

## Geography and Access

Kebris sits on [[Larhorak]], the largest of the [[Sentran Shields]]. Its position gives it access to the eastern defensive waters around [[Sentra]] and [[Kallindra]].

The city is reached primarily by sea. Its access depends on harbours, pilots, patrol routes, lighthouse chains, and wartime travel permissions. The nearby watch island holding [[The Sentry]] likely supports navigation and surveillance around [[Larhorak]].

## Economy and Function

Kebris likely functions as the main commercial, administrative, and logistical center of [[Larhorak]].

Its economy probably rests on:

- harbour trade
- ship provisioning
- fishing
- lighthouse and pilot services
- local markets
- military supply
- taxes from shield-island traffic
- storage and redistribution within the Shields

It should not compete with [[Kallindra]] directly. [[Kallindra]] is too large and central. Kebris is important within the shield system, not above it.

## Politics and Power

Kebris was once a city-state. That matters. The city may now be formally inside Falhaezrian political structures, but old civic families, merchant houses, harbour authorities, and local magistrates likely still remember its former independence.

The current political order probably includes:

- crown-appointed officials
- old civic families
- harbour authorities
- local councils
- military representatives
- merchant interests
- possible Aldermark-linked prestige

Kebris has been swallowed by the wider state, but that does not mean the city is culturally passive. Its people may accept crown protection while resenting crown supervision.

## History

Kebris began as a city-state on [[Larhorak]]. Over time, it was drawn fully into wider Falhaezrian politics.

[[Eldric of House Aldermark]] made his mark on [[Larhorak]], which gives the island and city a possible founder-line connection. That connection is not yet developed enough to define Kebris’s full political identity, but it should remain visible as a thread.

## Current Tensions

Kebris’s best tension is not open rebellion. It is absorbed pride.

Possible current tensions:

- old city-state families resent crown officials
- wartime restrictions damage harbour income
- military inspections slow trade
- [[Kallindra]] dominates commerce
- local citizens feel used as shield-island manpower
- crown authorities distrust Kebris’s old civic networks
- merchants quietly evade port controls
