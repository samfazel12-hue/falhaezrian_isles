## Overview

Tek Tenekka is the oldest city west of the [[Moonhills]] and the main survivor of pre-Béornic western culture. It was spared during the retaliatory campaigns after the [[Western Betrayal]].

## Geography and Access

Located in the [[Lake Tenekka]] district, tied to the lake and western road-water routes.

## Economy and Function

Cultural preservation, lake trade, records, old architecture, local administration.

## Politics and Power

Protected by the House during the retaliation because the [[Duke of Tenekka]] remained loyal and supplied intelligence. This protection makes the city both privileged and burdened.

## Key Locations

- old wooden pre-Béornic districts
- local religious sites 
- ducal records 

## Key Figures

- [[Duke of Tenekka]]

## History

Spared when much of the west burned. Now carries what the other western cities lost.
