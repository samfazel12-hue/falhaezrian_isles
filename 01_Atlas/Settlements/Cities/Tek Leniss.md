**Region:** [[Western Sentra]]
**Type:** city
**Status:** ruined / partially replaced by port memory

## Overview

Tek Leniss began as a daughter-settlement of [[Tek Tenekka]] and later outgrew it. It was destroyed during the retaliatory campaigns after the [[Western Betrayal]].

## Geography and Access

Near the [[Gulf of Lennis]] with better landing access and road links than [[Tek Tenekka]].

## Economy and Function

Formerly a stronger landing and trade settlement than its parent town.

## Politics and Power

Destroyed; political authority replaced by memory, ruin, and the surviving port community at [[Lennishaven]].

## History

Massacred in the western retaliations. The [[Gulf of Lennis]] ran red.
