**Region:** [[Southern Sentra]]
**Type:** city
**Status:** active

## Overview

Culverno is the largest majority-Ignahuatl city, built beside [[Mount Vulka]] and the old caldera. It is politically less dominant than [[Kallindra]] or [[Rhodokra]] but culturally central to the [[Ignahuatl]] south.

## Geography and Access

Located at the edge of the volcanic caldera near [[Mount Vulka]] and the [[Broken Forest]] glass plains.

## Economy and Function

Controls serpentglass craft and weapons. Religious pilgrimage, volcanic craft, and specialist heat-working define the city.

## Politics and Power

Technically subject to Sentran authority but practically independent in day-to-day rule because of its religious centrality and serpentglass monopoly.

## Key Locations

- [[Mount Vulka]]
- serpentglass workshops
- fire pantheon temples

## History

Bound to the [[Dragon War]], the volcanic underworld, and the [[Ignahuatl]] fire pantheon.
