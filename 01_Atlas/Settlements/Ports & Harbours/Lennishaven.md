## Overview

Lennishaven is the surviving port settlement near [[Tek Leniss]]. It preserved the folk memory of the [[Gulf of Lennis]] massacre when official histories did not.

## Geography and Access

On the eastern shore of the [[Gulf of Lennis]], tied to the destroyed city above or nearby.

## Economy and Function

Fishing, dock work, small port trade, oral history, and local survival.

## Politics and Power

Likely suspicious of official House narratives. Authority may be practical and communal rather than noble-heavy.
