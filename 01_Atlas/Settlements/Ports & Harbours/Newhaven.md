**Region:** [[Kallindra]]
**Type:** port district
**Status:** active

## Overview

Newhaven is [[Kallindra]]’s second-oldest port and [[Oldenhaven]]’s great rival. Its district houses the Thieves’ Guild.

## Geography and Access

Part of [[Kallindra]]’s port system.

## Economy and Function

Trade, smuggling, shadow logistics, dockside brokerage.

## Politics and Power

Formal commerce and criminal power overlap.

- Thieves’ Guild
