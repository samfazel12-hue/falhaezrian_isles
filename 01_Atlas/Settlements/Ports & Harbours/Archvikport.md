**Region:** [[Surgemark]]
**Type:** port settlement
**Status:** active

## Overview

Archvikport keeps [[Surgemark]]’s military base functioning. It handles provisioning, repair, personnel movement, and civilian life under the shadow of [[Wrackholm]].

## Geography and Access

On [[Surgemark]], tied directly to the harbour used by the naval base.

## Economy and Function

Provisioning, repair, transport, garrison supply.

## Politics and Power

Subordinate to the island’s military value and contested sovereignty.
