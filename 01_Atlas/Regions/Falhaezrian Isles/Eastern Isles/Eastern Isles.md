## Overview

The Eastern Isles form the political, military, institutional, and logistical core of the [[Falhaezrian Isles]].

The region contains [[Sentra]], [[Kallindra]], the [[Sentran Shields]], [[Ulum]], the [[Red Sea of Ulum]], and the main institutions through which the realm is ruled. The [[High King]], [[House of Béorn]], the two High House councils, the major eastern ports, the mountain redoubts, the shield islands, and much of the wartime supply system all sit inside this region.

The Eastern Isles are the part of the archipelago where central authority is strongest and most visible.

## Geography

The region is built around [[Sentra]] and the waters around it.

Sentra contains the [[Kazaros]], the [[Great Pearl]], the [[Sentran Breadbasket]], the [[Moonhills]], [[Western Sentra]], [[Southern Sentra]], [[Kallindra]], and the main inland routes of the state.

Around Sentra lie the [[Sentran Shields]], including [[Larhorak]], [[Amberhorn]], [[Raventide]], [[The Moody Rock]], [[The Lonely Rock]], and [[King's Rest]]. These islands guard the maritime approaches to [[Sentra]] and [[Kallindra]].

To the north lie [[Ulum]] and the [[Red Sea of Ulum]]. The northern system connects to Sentra through cold sea routes, major straits, difficult ports, and deep tunnels beneath the mountains and sea.

## Political Status

The Eastern Isles are ruled by [[House of Béorn]] and contain the main state machinery of the realm.

Central authority is strongest here, but it is not uniform. [[Sentra]] is the crown’s core. [[Kallindra]] is indispensable and politically complex. [[Rhodokra]] keeps older autonomy. [[Culverno]] holds practical independence through religion and serpentglass. [[Western Sentra]] remains bitter after the [[Western Betrayal]]. [[Ulum]] cooperates at the surface but remains separate and partly unknowable.

The region functions because law, councils, ports, roads, tunnels, military command, and common fear of the [[Continental Empire]] hold it together.
