## Overview

The Sentran Shields are the eastern defensive and maritime island field around [[Sentra]] and [[Kallindra]].

They include [[Larhorak]], [[Amberhorn]], [[Raventide]], [[The Moody Rock]], [[The Lonely Rock]], [[King's Rest]], and smaller watch-islands such as [[The Sentry]].

The Shields protect the approaches to [[Kallindra]], monitor eastern waters, guide ships toward safe harbours, and preserve several older political scars from the Continental invasions.

## Political Status

The Shields are under the authority of [[House of Béorn]], but each island has its own local history.

[[Larhorak]] and [[Kebris]] preserve the memory of city-state autonomy. [[Amberhorn]] is small but strategically important. [[Raventide]] is tied closely to Kallindra. [[The Moody Rock]] is House property and arcane territory. [[King's Rest]] has royal symbolism. [[The Lonely Rock]] remains a scar of invasion and enslavement.

The Shields are therefore not a single ordinary province. They are a defensive system made from islands with different legal, military, civic, arcane, and royal statuses.

## Military Role

The Shields are not meant to stop every enemy at sea. They make approach visible, risky, and slow.

Their defensive value comes from:

- lighthouse control
- watchtower reports
- island garrisons
- local pilots
- harbour chains
- signal fires
- restricted channels
- early warning to [[Kallindra]]
- control of safe approaches
- denial of enemy anchorages
- coordination with the [[Royal Navy]] 
- memory of failures such as [[The Lonely Rock]]

## History

The Shields developed as the maritime warning and defensive field around Sentra and Kallindra.

[[Larhorak]] became the largest island of the system, with [[Kebris]] growing from city-state into an absorbed political centre. [[Amberhorn]] developed an outsized role through [[Clawpoint]], whose light guided ships toward Kallindra and whose tower watched for golden-hulled vessels from [[Yarrowgold]].

During the [[Second Continental Invasion]], [[The Lonely Rock]] was sacked and used as an Imperial base for the Eastern Campaign. This turned the island into proof that the Shields could fail.

[[King's Rest]] later became a royal fortified island through [[Daran’s Tower]]. [[The Moody Rock]] developed separately as House property and arcane ground through the [[Mages’ House]].

## Strategic Value

The Shields protect the eastern heart of the realm.

Their value comes from:

- controlling approach to [[Kallindra]]
- protecting major ports
- warning against pirate or Continental movement
- supporting the [[Royal Navy]] 
- preserving safe routes through shoals and channels
- housing royal, arcane, and civic institutions
- preventing enemy use of eastern anchorages
- remembering and correcting the failure of [[The Lonely Rock]]
