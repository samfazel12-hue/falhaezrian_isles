# [[The Moody Rock]]

**Parent region:** [[Sentran Shields]]
**Controlled by:** [[House of Béorn]]
**Primary terrain:** isolated shield island, arcane institution, rocky coast

## Overview

[[The Moody Rock]] is an eastern island in the [[Sentran Shields]] and the home of the [[Mages’ House]]. It is property of [[House of Béorn]], but it is formally not part of the SDS, the [[Sentran Defensive Shields]], and it does not house official military bases.

That distinction matters. The island belongs to the shield chain geographically, but its function is arcane and institutional rather than openly military.

## Geography

[[The Moody Rock]] is isolated, weather-beaten, and controlled. It is not just “an island with a wizard school”; its geography separates arcane work from ordinary politics.

Its coastline is probably difficult enough to regulate access. The island’s name suggests a place shaped by weather, mood, sea conditions, and reputation.

## People and Culture

The dominant institution is the [[Mages’ House]], founded by [[Krike the Mage]], daughter of [[Béorn]] and [[Rosaria]].

The island’s culture should be institutional, guarded, scholarly, and strange. People who live there likely belong to the arcane order, support staff, boat crews, scribes, servants, wardens, or House officials assigned to the property.

## Political Status

[[The Moody Rock]] is House property. This makes it politically sensitive.

It is not formally part of the military shield system, but that does not mean it is politically harmless. An arcane institution founded by a Bloodroyal daughter and held by the House is a strategic asset even if no soldiers are stationed there.

## Settlements

- [[Mages’ House]]: arcane institution founded by [[Krike the Mage]].

## Key Geography

- **Institution:** [[Mages’ House]]
- **Nearby region:** eastern shield waters
- **Status:** House property, not part of the formal SDS
- **Access:** likely controlled by boat, permits, and institutional authority

## History

[[The Moody Rock]] is tied to [[Krike the Mage]], one of the Bloodroyal daughters. Krike belongs to the arcane foundations of the setting and is linked to the discovery of Vulka.

The island’s history should therefore connect arcane research, House authority, and older Bloodroyal legitimacy.

## Routes and Barriers

Access is probably restricted. Travel to the island would require approval from the House, the [[Mages’ House]], or both.

The main barriers are institutional, magical, and maritime.

- magical research
- rare texts or instruments
