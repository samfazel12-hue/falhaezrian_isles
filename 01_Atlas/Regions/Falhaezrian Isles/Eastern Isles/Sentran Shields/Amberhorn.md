**Parent region:** [[Sentran Shields]]
**Controlled by:** [[House of Béorn]] / wider Falhaezrian state
**Primary terrain:** small shield island, coastal watchland, wildlife reserve

## Overview

Amberhorn is the smallest island of the [[Sentran Shields]]. Its importance is not size but position. It holds [[Clawpoint]], a fort, watchtower, and lighthouse that guides ships toward [[Featherport]] and the [[Port of the People]].

Amberhorn is compact, exposed, windy, and watched: the kind of island where every landing matters.

## Geography

Amberhorn lies in the eastern shield chain around [[Sentra]] and [[Kallindra]]. Its coastline is probably broken enough to need lighthouse guidance, but not large enough to support major inland settlement.

The island’s key site is [[Clawpoint]], which combines military, navigational, and more recent scientific or conservation use.

## People and Culture

Amberhorn’s population is probably small. It likely consists of lighthouse keepers, watch personnel, garrison families, marine workers, reserve staff, and a few local fishing households.

Because [[Clawpoint]] now also houses marine biologists, Amberhorn has a useful contrast: old military watch culture beside newer conservation and scientific work.

## Political Status

Amberhorn is part of the Sentran shield system and therefore falls under central strategic oversight. It is too small to be politically independent, but its lighthouse and watch function make it more important than its population suggests.

## Settlements

- [[Clawpoint]]: fort, watchtower, lighthouse, and wildlife reserve station.

## Key Geography

- **Fortifications:** [[Clawpoint]]
- **Waters:** approaches toward [[Featherport]] and [[Port of the People]]
- **Coast:** lighthouse-relevant shoals, exposed rocks, bird cliffs, marine reserve waters

## History

[[Clawpoint]] historically watched for ships with golden hulls from [[Yarrowgold]]. That makes Amberhorn part of the long maritime suspicion between [[Sentra]] and the southern powers.

In recent years, [[Clawpoint]] has also become a wildlife reserve with its own marine biologists. That is good lore. Keep it. It prevents the island from being just another generic watch post.

## Routes and Barriers

Amberhorn matters to ships approaching [[Kallindra]]’s port system. Its lighthouse function means it sits along practical maritime routes, not just symbolic defensive lines.

Barriers include rough seas, restricted wartime navigation, and possibly protected marine zones.

## Strategic Value

Amberhorn is a small island with an oversized navigational role. Whoever controls its light and watchtower can affect movement toward key Kallindran harbours.

## Resources

- lighthouse service
- watch personnel
- marine research
- wildlife reserve value
- fishing and coastal knowledge
- warning capacity against southern or pirate ships
