**Parent region:** [[Sentran Shields]]
**Controlled by:** [[House of Béorn]] / wider Falhaezrian state
**Primary terrain:** shield island, coastal settlements, fortified approaches

## Overview

Larhorak is the largest island of the [[Sentran Shields]]. It is one of the main eastern shield islands around [[Sentra]] and [[Kallindra]], and its size gives it more political and demographic weight than most islands in the chain.

The island is important because it holds [[Kebris]], the largest city in the Shields, and because it is tied to the legacy of [[Eldric of House Aldermark]]. It began with a stronger city-state identity, but has since been drawn fully into wider Falhaezrian politics.

## Geography

Larhorak lies east of the central Sentran sphere and forms part of the outer maritime field protecting the eastern approaches. Its coast is strategically exposed: visible to ships, useful for warning, and vulnerable during invasion or pirate pressure.

The small island holding [[The Sentry]] lies off Larhorak and functions as a watchtower and lighthouse position.

## People and Culture

Larhorak’s people are likely more maritime and outward-facing than inland Sentrans. They live with the sea as a defensive frontier, trade route, and danger zone.

[[Kebris]] probably dominates the island’s identity. Older local city-state pride may still exist, but it has been absorbed into the institutions of the Falhaezrian realm.

## Political Status

Larhorak is no longer a free city-state sphere. It has been swallowed into the central political order of the Isles.

This should create quiet tension. Old civic families in [[Kebris]] may remember a time when the island’s decisions were made locally. The crown likely treats Larhorak as too important to leave politically loose.

## Settlements

- [[Kebris]]: largest city in the [[Sentran Shields]].
- [[The Sentry]]: watchtower and lighthouse on a small island off Larhorak.

## Key Geography

- **Islands:** [[The Sentry]]
- **Cities:** [[Kebris]]
- **Waters:** eastern shield waters
- **Fortifications:** coastal watch positions, lighthouse stations

## History

Larhorak is linked to [[Eldric of House Aldermark]], who made his mark on the island. [[Kebris]] was originally a city-state but has become part of wider Falhaezrian politics.

This makes Larhorak useful for exploring the difference between local civic memory and central state absorption.

## Routes and Barriers

Larhorak controls or watches part of the eastern maritime route system. Movement is by ship, coastal road, and harbour network.

The main barriers are not mountains or forests, but sea exposure, naval patrols, storms, pirates, and wartime restrictions.

## Strategic Value

Larhorak’s value comes from size, settlement, and position. It is a shield island in the literal political geography of the realm: part warning post, part defensive belt, part civic island, part naval stepping stone.

## Resources

- harbour labour
- coastal trade
- fishing
- civic taxes from [[Kebris]]
- lighthouse and watch infrastructure
- possible ship repair and provisioning
