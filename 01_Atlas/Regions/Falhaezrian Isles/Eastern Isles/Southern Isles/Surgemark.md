**Parent region:** Southern Isles
**Controlled by:** contested: [[Sentra]] / [[Yarrowgold]]
**Primary terrain:** crescent island and naval base

## Overview

Surgemark is a contested crescent island south of [[Sentra]]. It matters because whoever holds it controls a forward naval base threatening the southern Sentran coast and Kallindran harbours.

## Geography

Crescent-shaped island in the [[Surgewater Gulf]].

## People and Culture

Garrison, port workers, sailors, repair crews, and civilians serving the island’s military function.

## Political Status

Changed hands repeatedly between [[Sentra]] and [[Yarrowgold]]. Its status stays active rather than settled.

## Settlements

[[Wrackholm]], [[Archvikport]]

## Key Geography

- Fort: [[Wrackholm]]
- Port: [[Archvikport]]

## History

Repeated treaties and wars failed to remove the island from dispute.

## Routes and Barriers

Southern approach to [[Sentra]] through the [[Surgewater Gulf]].

## Strategic Value

Forward naval base and southern choke point.

## Resources

Harbour, repair, provisioning, military anchorage.
