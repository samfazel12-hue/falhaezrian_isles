**Parent region:** [[Eastern Isles]]
**Controlled by:** [[Ulum]] / House enclaves
**Primary terrain:** cold sea, red mountains, dwarven coast

## Overview

[[Ulum]] and the Red Sea form the cold northern frontier between [[Sentra]] and the dwarven mountain world. The surface dwarves maintain practical relations with The House, while deeper [[Ulum]] remains dangerous and poorly known.

## Geography

Bitter northern water, glacial melt, winter ice, difficult ports, red mountain coast, deep tunnel systems below.

## People and Culture

Surface dwarves, mountain clans, coastal dwarves, and deeper duergar-related peoples below.

## Political Status

Cooperation exists at the surface. [[Redcliff]] remains a legal House property on [[Ulum]] and a permanent irritant.

- Mountains: Red Mountains
- Underground: [[Deep Ulum]]

## History

Dwarves taught The House tunneling knowledge. The deep networks later confirmed that the underworld beneath [[Sentra]] and [[Ulum]] connects.
