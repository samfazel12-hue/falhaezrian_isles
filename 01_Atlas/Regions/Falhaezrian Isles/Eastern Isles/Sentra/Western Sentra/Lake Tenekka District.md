**Parent region:** [[Western Sentra]]
**Controlled by:** [[Duke of Tenekka]] / western Sentran authorities
**Primary terrain:** lake district

## Overview

The [[Lake Tenekka]] District is the surviving cultural core of western [[Sentra]]. [[Tek Tenekka]] preserved old western architecture, language, and religion after much of the west was destroyed.

## Geography

Built around [[Lake Tenekka]] and linked to the [[Gulf of Lennis]] through nearby settlements and ports.

## People and Culture

[[Tènnakkannin]] communities, survivors, dockworkers, and local noble authority.

## Political Status

Loyalty to The House is complicated by survival, guilt, and the memory of western devastation.

## Settlements

[[Tek Tenekka]], [[Tek Leniss]], [[Lennishaven]]

## Key Geography

- Lake: [[Lake Tenekka]]
- Coast: [[Gulf of Lennis]]

## History

Spared during the [[Western Betrayal]]’s retaliation because the [[Duke of Tenekka]] aided The House.

## Routes and Barriers

Lake movement, road links, port access at [[Lennishaven]].

## Strategic Value

A rare surviving western cultural archive and a possible bridge between west and House power.

## Resources

Lake trade, old records, western legitimacy.
