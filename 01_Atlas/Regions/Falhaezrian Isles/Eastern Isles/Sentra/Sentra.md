## Overview

[[Sentra]] is the political, sacred, military, and institutional heart of the [[Falhaezrian Isles]].

It contains [[Skystone]], [[Kacore]], the High House, the [[First High House Council|First House]], the [[Béornic]] heartland, [[Rhodokra]], [[Kallindra]], and several older cultural regions that predate [[Béornic]] unification.

The island is formally united under [[House of Béorn]], but its regions differ strongly in culture, loyalty, terrain, and political weight.

## Geography

Sentra is shaped by the [[Kazaros]] mountains, the [[Great Pearl]] basin, the [[Moonhills]], the [[Broken Forest]], western lakes, northern cold waters, and the southern volcanic country around [[Mount Vulka]].

Movement across the island is controlled by passes, gates, ports, river corridors, lake routes, military roads, marshes, and old regional borders.

## Political Status

Sentra is ruled by [[House of Béorn]] through royal authority, council government, fortified routes, old house obligations, port control, and wartime emergency powers.

Control is strongest in the [[Kazaros]], the eastern heartland, and core House institutions. It is more complicated in [[Rhodokra]], [[Kallindra]], [[Western Sentra]], and [[Southern Sentra]].

## History

[[Béorn]] unified Sentra after defeating Krova at [[Siege of Twixtriver]].

Later eras added Bloodroyal houses, council government, the [[Bloodroyal War]], Continental invasions, the [[Dragon War]], the [[Western Betrayal]], western retaliatory campaigns, and present wartime centralization.

The island still carries older divisions beneath the House order.
