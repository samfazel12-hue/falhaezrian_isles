## Overview

Southern Sentra is the [[Ignahuatl]] region around [[Mount Vulka]], [[Culverno]], [[Broken Forest]], serpentglass, and the southern volcanic coast.

The region is formally part of [[Sentra]] and subject to [[House of Béorn]], but [[Culverno]] holds strong practical independence through religious authority and control of serpentglass craft.

Southern Sentra is culturally distinct from the [[Béornic]] heartland. Its religious life is oriented downward, toward heat, pressure, magma, smoke, caldera, underworld passages, and fire beneath the world.

## Geography

Southern Sentra is shaped by an active volcano, an old caldera, volcanic passages, vitrified forest scars, southern roads, coastal access, and the boundary with central Sentra.

The main geographic anchors are [[Mount Vulka]], [[Culverno]], the [[Broken Forest]], [[Last Stand]], [[Lordsgate]], and the [[Vulkan Gulf]].

## Political Status

Southern Sentra is under [[House of Béorn]] in law, but [[Culverno]] controls much of the region in practice.

Culverno’s independence rests on two pillars:

| Pillar | Function |
|---|---|
| Religious centrality | Culverno is the centre of the Ignahuatl fire pantheon and the southern sacred order. |
| Serpentglass monopoly | No other city combines access to the glass plains with the craft tradition needed to work serpentglass. |

The crown needs southern materials and routes, but cannot easily dominate Culverno without disturbing Ignahuatl religious authority and the serpentglass economy.

## Ignahuatl Fire Pantheon

The Ignahuatl faith is a chthonic fire religion. It is distinct from sun worship elsewhere in the Isles.

| Aspect | Domain |
|---|---|
| Forge-fire | controlled heat, craft, useful flame, tools, weapons. |
| Raw magma | destructive heat, eruption, uncontrolled force. |
| Caldera | sleeping danger, contained pressure, fire that has not spoken. |
| Smoke and gas | invisible killing power, fumes, poisoned air, hidden heat. |
| Older depth  | something beneath or behind the known aspects, tied to the deep world below Vulka. |

The faith looks downward rather than skyward. Its core concerns are heat from below, transformation, pressure, destruction, creation, and the interior of the world.

## Serpentglass

[[Serpentglass]] is dragonfire-vitrified soil harvested from the glass plains of the [[Broken Forest]].

| Property | Notes |
|---|---|
| Origin | soil and surface material vitrified by dragonfire. |
| Source | [[Broken Forest Glass Plains]]. |

| Uses | blades, tools, weapons, ritual objects . |
| Prized form | macuahuitl-style wooden weapon edged with multiple serpentglass blades. |
| Trade value | high value in [[Kallindra]] and beyond. |
| Name status | “Serpentglass” is common usage, even if contested. |

- Ignahuatl religious authority

- [[Lordsgate]] staging

- possible Underdark routes

Control of Southern Sentra is not only territorial. It is control over a sacred material economy and a dangerous volcanic-underworld threshold.
