## Overview

The Falhaezrian Isles are a large maritime realm centred on [[Sentra]], [[Kallindra]], and the eastern shield islands.

The realm is ruled through the [[High King]], [[House of Béorn]], the [[First High House Council]], and the [[Second High House Council]]. This central order binds many islands and peoples while leaving local law, taxation, religious authority, and regional custom partly intact.

The Isles are unified by sea routes, councils, shared enemies, old oaths, port control, House law, and fear of the [[Continental Empire]]. Their unity is real, but uneven.

## Political Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| House service over blood | [[House of Béorn]] admits by merit, loyalty, service, skill, learning, craft, or achievement. |
| Selected High Kingship | Succession is recognized by capacity and council support, not strict automatic inheritance. |
| Layered rule | Central law coexists with local law, taxation, and custom. |
| Emergency authority | War expands royal power over ports, movement, taxation, supply, and military command. |
| Council tension | The [[Second High House Council|Second House]] gives motion; the [[First High House Council|First House]] gives restraint. |
| Maritime unity | Ports, straits, convoys, and naval corridors hold the Isles together. |
| Regional incompleteness | Western representation and outer regions remain politically unresolved. |
